## The Easiest Way to Share Inspiring Quotes on WordPress

> **QuoteFrameShare** helps you add beautiful, customizable blockquotes to your posts and pages—complete with citation, copy-to-clipboard, and one-click social sharing.  
> No bloat, no tracking, just a fast, modern block that works with any theme.

---

## Why Choose QuoteFrameShare?

- **Save Time:** Instantly add stylish, responsive blockquotes with just a few clicks.
- **Boost Engagement:** Let visitors copy or share your quotes on Facebook, WhatsApp, Telegram, and X (Twitter).
- **Look Professional:** Customize typography, colors, borders, and more to match your brand.
- **Trustworthy:** 100% open source, no tracking, no upsells, no external assets.
- **SEO & Accessibility:** Clean markup, accessible controls, and fully responsive design.

---

## Features

- **Gutenberg Block:** Add blockquotes anywhere in the editor.
- **Customizable Styles:** Background, text, border, shadow, padding, margin, and max width.
- **Separate Typography:** Control fonts for both quote and citation.
- **Citation Field:** Optional, for proper attribution.
- **Copy to Clipboard:** With instant feedback alert.
- **Social Share Buttons:** Facebook, WhatsApp, Telegram, X (Twitter) – enable/disable as needed.
- **Font Awesome Icons:** Choose your icon, always in official brand colors.
- **Mobile Friendly:** Inline icons, touch-friendly, and fully responsive.
- **Multilingual:** Works with any language or script.
- **No External Requests:** All assets are bundled for privacy and speed.

---

## How It Works

1. **Add the Block:** Insert the QuoteFrameShare block in the Gutenberg editor.
2. **Enter Your Quote:** Type your quote and (optionally) a citation.
3. **Customize:** Use the sidebar to adjust styles, typography, and which social icons appear.
4. **Share or Copy:** Visitors can copy the quote or share it on social media with one click.

---

## Documentation

### Block Settings

- **Settings Panel:**  
  Enable/disable social icons, select Font Awesome icon, toggle brand colors, copy button, typography, and block colors.
- **Style Panel:**  
  Adjust padding, margin, border radius, border style, shadow, and max width.

### Social Sharing

- **Facebook:** Shares the quote and URL.
- **WhatsApp:** Shares the quote and URL (mobile-friendly).
- **Telegram:** Shares the quote and URL.
- **X (Twitter):** Shares the quote and URL.

### Copy to Clipboard

- Copies the quote (and citation, if present) to the clipboard.
- Shows a “Copied!” alert for user feedback.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I customize the block style?**  
A: Yes! You can change background, text, border, shadow, padding, margin, and typography for both quote and citation.

**Q: Can I disable some social icons?**  
A: Yes, toggle each icon in the block settings.

**Q: Does it support Hindi or Unicode text?**  
A: Yes, you can use any language or script in your quotes and citations.

**Q: Will it slow down my site?**  
A: No. QuoteFrameShare is lightweight, loads only what’s needed, and uses no external assets.

**Q: Is it compatible with my theme?**  
A: Yes, it works with any modern WordPress theme.

## Installation

1. Download or clone this repository.
2. Upload the `quoteframeshare` folder to your WordPress `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
3. Activate the plugin via the WordPress admin.
4. Add the QuoteFrameShare block in the Gutenberg editor.
